Our Mission: To Focus on supplying our families, local communities, with nutritious farm-grown food while regenerating the land we care for.

Located in the foothills of the Cascasde Mountains above Scotts Mills, in the central Willamette Valley in Oregon, lies our home and the Lazy S and J Farm.
Here, we specialize in preserving the rare Meishan Pig; currently listed as Threatened by The Livestock Conservancy, our Apariary of locally queened honey bees, boer goats, rabbits and chickens.

About our animals

All of our animals are raised on our pastures using resilient and regenerative land management practices with a focus on addressing all things on our farm holistically.

Our Products

We pride ourselves in beeing truly "Farm to Table" through our practices of managing and processing everything we sell on our farm. By doing this, we can ensure the highest quality products and responsiveness to our customers' needs.

For example:

  • Our animals are selectively bread for the quality traits and standards we have identified. By doing this we can mazimize our resources and time raising the best animals.
  • We then will process these animals our on farm, custom built facility, that was designed up hold our high standards for quality, effeciency and adapability for our products today as well as for years to come.
  • Our customers are able to see the whole process of where their food comes from, ask questions and provide us direct feedback.
  • By managing our products in this way we can strive to be the best community partner we can.
